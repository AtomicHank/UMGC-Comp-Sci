Week 1

Software engineering is defined as a broad field  that touches on all aspects of developing and supporting a software system.

In this discussion, you will select two principles of the field of software engineering. You will explain the meaning of your selected principles and how they are applied in developing and supporting software systems.

To prepare for this discussion:

1) Review the module's learning resources and its references.

To complete this discussion:

1) Explain the meaning of two principles of software engineering of your choice.
2) Explain how your two selected principles are applied in developing and supporting software systems.
