Unit 1
This is an ungraded discussion, however it is crucial that students complete this discussion and inform the instructor of the team formation. For this discussion, team members should form a group and select a project idea that they would like to work on. Team members should brainstorm the project scope and vision and come up with a project overview. 

One member of each team should post the names of the group members for the group, including the topic for the selected project and a short project overview in the format given below. Each of the members named in that team simply needs to respond that they agree to being in the group, or let the group know they are already in another group.

Project Topic: (Name of your project)
Team members: (List of group members)
Project Overview: (A paragraph including the problem/needs your project would be addressing, goal of your project/software, who will be using the project/software)

Unit 2
For this unit’s discussion, complete the assigned readings for the unit on Project Planning and discuss the key concepts and insights you have gained. In your discussion, include the essential elements of a project plan and reflect on the critical components that should be included in a well-defined project plan.

You can base your discussion on your group's project plan, including (but not limited to) the following topics:

How do you define the goals, objectives and deliverables of your project
What are the limitations of your project
How do you assign responsibilities to team members
How do you develop project timeline and milestones
What resources will be required for your project
What tools you will be using for communication among team members
Engage in thoughtful debates and challenge each other's perspectives, encouraging a rich and dynamic exchange of ideas. Respond to two of your classmates' posts by providing insight into their observations.
